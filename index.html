Risk Assessment Rating System:

  • MODERATE: Unvaccinated travelers who are at increased risk for severe illness should avoid travel to this destination.
  • HIGH: Unvaccinated travelers should avoid nonessential travel to this destination.
  • SEVERE: Avoid travel to this destination.
